Welcome to Hai Gau Coffee

Coffee Hai Gau was created for people who love coffee and relaxing spaces. Our café is a place where friends can meet, students can study, and everyone can enjoy a peaceful moment with a cup of coffee.

We carefully select high-quality coffee beans and craft every drink with passion and attention to detail. Our goal is to create not only delicious coffee but also a warm and welcoming experience for every customer who visits our café.
